Pan & Tilt mechanism

Parts list required:
2 Servos Futaba S3003
1 Ultrasonic ranging module HC - SR04
1 $3 USB webcam
M1.6 x 10mm screws and nuts

Original files head.stl and servo.stl were repaired using NetFabb (https://netfabb.azurewebsites.net/) and sliced using Slic3r (http://http://slic3r.org/).
